# Sales Dashboard (Excel)

### Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance across categories, time periods, and regions.
The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders quickly understand key business metrics, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

It focuses on clarity, usability, and business relevance, making it suitable for recruiters and business users alike.

### Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

Analyze overall sales and profit performance

Compare category-wise and sub-category-wise sales

Track profit trends over time

Visualize regional performance using geographic insights

Enable quick filtering by Category and Year

### Key Features

Category-wise Sales Analysis
Visual breakdown of sales across sub-categories such as Phones, Chairs, Storage, and Accessories.

Profit Trend Over Time
Line chart showing profit comparison across categories for different years.

Geographical Sales Distribution
Map visualization highlighting sales performance across different U.S. states.

Year-wise Comparison
Clear comparison of performance metrics between selected years.

Interactive Slicers
Dynamic filtering by:

Category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)

Year (2019, 2020)

### Dashboard Insights

Technology category contributes significantly to overall profit growth.

Sales show a noticeable increase in the later months of the year.

Certain states consistently outperform others in terms of total sales.

Office Supplies maintain steady growth compared to other categories.
